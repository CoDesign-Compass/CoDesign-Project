# Sprint 02 – Retrospective PBIs

This document tracks Product Backlog Items (PBIs) created from the latest Sprint Retrospective.  
Each PBI represents an actionable improvement for the team.  

---

## PBIs

### PBI-1: Streamline Meeting Process
- **Description:** As a team member, I want meetings to follow a structured agenda (review tasks, plan new tasks, clarify questions) so that we can finish within ~30 minutes.  
- **Priority:** High  
- **Owner:** Team (Scrum Master to facilitate)  
- **Target Sprint:** Next Sprint  

---

### PBI-2: Improve Communication on Design Consistency
- **Description:** As a developer, I want clear communication and confirmation on design decisions so that the prototype and final product remain consistent.  
- **Priority:** Medium  
- **Owner:** Team  
- **Target Sprint:** Ongoing, from next Sprint  

---

### PBI-3: Enforce Checklist Discipline
- **Description:** As a team, we want to review the sprint checklist item by item before submission so that we meet all requirements and improve our sprint score.  
- **Priority:** High  
- **Owner:** Team  
- **Target Sprint:** Next Sprint  

---

## Notes
- These PBIs are process improvements (not product features).  
- They should be tracked together with product backlog items, either via Issues (recommended) or as documentation here.
