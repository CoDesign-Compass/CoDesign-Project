## Product Vision
*FOR* people with lived experience of policy and services, such as carers and community members of diverse backgrounds,  
*WHO* want to share their perspectives in a safe, inclusive, and accessible way,  
*THE* CoDesignCompass website is a secure, interactive feedback platform  
*THAT* guides participants from their personal experiences to structured “why” and “how” reflections, ensuring their voices inform real policy and service design and helping address the issues they care about.  
*UNLIKE* traditional consultations or rigid surveys that are time-consuming, one-way, and often dominated by louder voices,  
*OUR* product prioritizes anonymity, inclusivity, and ease of use—allowing both tech-savvy advocates and cautious, non-technical participants to contribute meaningfully. The website provides real-time visual feedback, incentives, and adaptive questioning that evolves with input, creating a transparent and motivating feedback loop.
